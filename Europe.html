Spain

Exploring Seville

If you enjoy exploring exotic cities all from the comfort of European borders, Seville, Spain is the place to be. Visit the Real Alcazar, a magnificent marriage of Christian and Mudejar architecture. More recently, the Alcazar featured as a location for the Game of Thrones TV series. The world's largest Gothic cathedral resides in Seville, built between 1434 and 1517, over the remains of what had previously been the city's main mosque. The momunmental tomb of Christopher Columbus also lays inside the Cathedral, tickets for student's cost €5 so don't forget your student ID.

Iceland

Discover Iceland

Many people put Iceland on their to do list, but never seem to find the right time to visit the northern Gem. Home to the famous Blue Lagoon spa, hot springs, the golden circle in Geysir and ofcourse the northern lights - Iceland is not to be missed. If creating a jam packed itenerary doesn't seem to be your forte, Extreme Iceland is a great place to start and will guarantee the lowest price when you book activities online.

Milan, Italy

Ruled by the Caesars, Napoléon, the Austro-Hungarians and Mussolini, Milan has an ancient and fascinating history. However if hiking or intense site seeing isn't your cup of tea and you have 48-72 hours to spare, head to Milan, Italy's Italy’s city of the future, a fast-paced metropolis where money talks, creativity is big business and looking good is an art form. Milan's cathedral lies within close proxemity to all your favourite stores, high street to designer.
