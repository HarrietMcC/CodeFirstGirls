The United Kingdom

England

London

London, one of the most visited cities in the world. Home to a great deal of multicultralism, representing 270 nationalities and 300 tongues spoken within the city. Central London is where the major museums, galleries and most iconic sights congregate, but visit Hampstead Heath or the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park to flee the crowds and frolic in wide open green expanses. You can also venture further out to Kew Gardens, Richmond or Hampton Court Palace for beautiful panoramas of riverside London followed by a pint in a quiet waterside pub.

Scotland's High Lands

From Hogwarts to the High Lands, Edinburgh is an underrated travel piece of art.
